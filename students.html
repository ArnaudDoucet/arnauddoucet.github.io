Oxford University Research group

The D^2 group (Deligiannidis*Doucet) was created around 2013-2014, working initially primarily on computational statistics then also on machine learning. The group has published numerous papers in the leading statistics journals and top ML conferences (about 30% being spotlight/oral) as well as papers in probability, control and signal processing but none here. Its last PhD student will graduate soon and the group will be closed for good.
